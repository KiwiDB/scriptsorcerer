Event Registration

  • My Role Designer, Developer

Technologies

  • Systems MySQL, Excel, Email
  • Languages HTML5, CSS3 (responsive), Javascript, PHP, MySQL, JSON
An application system I set up for Hobsons to manage session registration for our London users' conference. Working with our UK team, the system was used primarily on-site with iPads and Hobsons supervision for information and registration modification purposes. Allows users to view information about sessions and register or change registration (given that the conditions allow the registration - room capacity, for example). Gives users a printable schedule of their registration, and sends email alerts to registrants as appropriate. The system also includes an admin-level mass registration process, reading registrant data from an Excel document.
