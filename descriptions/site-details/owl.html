A Joomla! based microsite (~16 pages) for The Online Western New York Learning Alliance (The OWL Group). I took a design created by a graphic designer and implemented it in Joomla!, adding my own thoughts, along with content provided by the organization and our content editors. Working alongside the account manager, I added a functioning contact form which integrates with other Hobsons systems using web services (SOAP).

This is one of a series of marketing microsites I created as part of Media Services team at Hobsons. Some others that I built:
