Import/Export Email Manager

Task Automation

  • My Role Project Manager, Developer, Designer

Technologies

  • Systems MySQL, IMAP, SimpleBrowser
  • Languages HTML5, CSS, Javascript, PHP, jQuery, MySQL
An internal automation tool I wrote for the Product Support team at Hobsons. The system accesses individual IMAP email accounts looking for import/export monitor alerts from our products. Once analyzed, the system can look up shared data and notes about clients and can send automated emails with exception details (consolidated from a series of external systems) to the appropriate account managers.
