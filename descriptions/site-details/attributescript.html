Technologies

An application written for several teams internally at Hobsons. This automation solution mass-creates fields in a Hobsons product by reading details from an Excel document. Data validations, both in the Excel document and in the client's instance of the associated Hobsons product are performed, and controls are provided to allow for customizations before execution.
