A project dashboard I wrote for the use of a team I led. The system generates content for Jira Issue Tracker issues to be sent daily to each of three sub teams, based on an initial client schedule and overall project due date. It also sends notification emails to the appropriate account managers as their client accounts move through the process. As the project manager, it gives me an at-a-glance view of the project as a whole, as well as an ability to see details about individual aspects of the project.
