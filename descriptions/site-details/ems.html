A Joomla-based website for the Enrollment Management Services (EMS) division of Hobsons. I took a design created by a graphic designer and implemented it in Joomla!, adding my own thoughts, along with content provided by the organization and our content editors. Also researched and created the main EMS blog, and used the RSS feed from the blog to display a preview of the most recent posts on the homepage. (Site is currently in "Phease 1", with "Phase 2" in development.)
